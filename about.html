ARTSanct

The Art Sanct Task Force’s goal is to track art owned by sanctioned individuals and prevent illegal trade in art and antiquities stolen by the occupiers in Ukraine during the war.

The Art Sanct Task Force will offer guidance, to create awareness among the members of the international art community, to ensure that the field remains compliant, in addition to maintaining a database of looted art and art owned by sanctioned persons, which are illegal for art businesses to transact.

As an integral player in the art world, your insights are of outmost importance to ensure implementation of these sanction regimes. We invite you to submit your feedback, suggestions, and questions by completing this survey.

While sanctions laws in different jurisdictions provide for strict liability for art businesses in transacting with sanctioned individuals, the Art Sanct Task Force aims to aid the art community by providing clear notice about which artworks may be affiliated with sanctioned individuals. We recognize that some art businesses may not be fully aware of the scope of sanction regimes.

We appreciate your assistance with this initiative.
